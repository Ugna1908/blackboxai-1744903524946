About Gokuldham Gau Sewa Mahatirth

A place specially designed with love and care towards cows.

Gokuldham Gau Sewa Mahatirth is a place that is specially designed with love and care towards cows. It was built under the grace of Sunil Nimana ji who is a visionary, having a vision of providing food, shelter and medicinal support to the cows and cattle that are deserted, hurt or have been in an accident.

It is the result of efforts of Sunil Nimana Ji that Gokuldham Gau Sewa Mahatirth has been able to open the biggest cow treatment hospital in Haryana. The Ganga Cow-Promotion Center has been established under the supervision of Gokuldham on the holy festival of Gopashtami. Every village is to become a Gaugram, according to the principal goal of this initiative.

Our Mission

We are committed to providing care, shelter, and medical treatment to cows across India.

Medical Care

Providing top-notch medical facilities for injured, sick, and abandoned cows, including complex surgeries like cesarean operations.

Safe Shelter

Creating a safe and nurturing environment for cows that have been abandoned, injured, or rescued from difficult situations.

Compassionate Care

Ensuring that every cow receives loving care, proper nutrition, and a dignified life in our shelter.

Our Services

Gokuldham has offered and continues to offer various services for cow welfare.

Caesarean Operation

Performing complex cesarean operations with a nearly 100% success rate, saving the lives of mother cows and their calves.

Rheunotomy Operation

Specialized surgical procedures to address digestive issues in cows, particularly those who have consumed harmful materials.

Medical Treatment

Providing medicines and treatments to more than 92,000 cows, addressing a wide range of health issues and conditions.

Rescue Operations

As soon as information is received, our ambulance runs to protect and rescue cows in distress, regardless of location or condition.

Major Surgeries

Performing big operations no matter how much time or money it takes, prioritizing the well-being of every cow.

Proper Nutrition

Ensuring all cows receive balanced and nutritious food to maintain their health and support their recovery.
